Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Student Management System Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our system.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal identification information (Name, Student ID, Email address)
  • Academic records and attendance data
  • Login credentials and account information
  • System usage data and activity logs

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Managing student records and academic progress
  • Tracking attendance and generating reports
  • Communication between students, teachers, and administrators
  • System improvement and user experience enhancement
  • Security and fraud prevention

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure data encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access controls
  • Secure backup systems

User Rights

As a user of our system, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Receive a copy of your data
  • Object to certain data processing activities

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may also retain and use your information as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any material changes through the system or via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Contact page.
